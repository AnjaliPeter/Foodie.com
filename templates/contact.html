Foodie.com

Foodie.com is India's largest online food ordering and delivery platform, founded in July 2014. Foodie.com is based in Bangalore, India, and as of September 2021, is operating in 500 Indian cities. In early 2019, Foodie.com expanded into general product deliveries under the name Foodie.com Stores. With the launch of Foodie.com, often dubbed as one of India’s fastest-growing companies, ordering food at the home, office, or even while throwing a party seems like a breeze. Foodie.com has been quite popular even in its early days, it witnessed an exemplary expansion and is carrying on its successful legacy till now!
